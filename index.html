Ashford Fight Club

Spar. Compete. Learn.

Ashford Fight Club is a community for anyone whose interested in learning or training any martial art. All forms of martial art are welcomed.
Every week we do a meet up for people to spar and learn from one another of their respective discipline.

The Founder of Ashford Fight Club

Greetings martial art enthusiasts, this is Sensei Takamura. I have been teaching Karate, Judo and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu for 14 years.
I created this community because I have been asked by many students of mine about different school of martial art and another
reason is because of Bruce Lee's philoshophy where you only take the things you find useful from each style.
